Achievement March 28, 2025

Akapaloo Academy Wins National Science & Maths Quiz Championship for the Third Consecutive Year

In a stunning display of academic brilliance, Akapaloo Academy's STEM team defeated 64 schools from across Ghana to claim the prestigious National Science and Maths Quiz Championship. The team, coached by Mr. Kofi Amponsah, answered correctly 98% of all questions in the final round, setting a new competition record. Headmaster Prof. Emmanuel Boateng called the victory "a reflection of our students' hard work, our teachers' dedication, and our school's unwavering commitment to excellence in STEM education."
