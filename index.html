Who We Are

A Legacy of Learning Since 1998

Akapaloo Academy was founded on the belief that every child deserves access to transformative education. For over two decades, we have been a pillar of academic excellence in our community — producing graduates who go on to lead in medicine, engineering, law, arts, and public service.

Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with co-curricular activities, mentorship, and values education to develop well-rounded individuals prepared for a rapidly changing world.
