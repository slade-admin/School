About Akapaloo Academy

Our story, our values, and the passionate people who make our school extraordinary.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

The guiding principles that shape every decision, lesson, and interaction at Akapaloo Academy.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide an inclusive, stimulating, and values-driven education that equips every student with the knowledge, skills, and character to lead purposeful lives and contribute meaningfully to society.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the foremost centre of academic excellence and character development in Africa — a community of lifelong learners and principled leaders who transform the world.

💎

Our Values

Excellence · Integrity · Respect · Innovation · Inclusivity · Service — these six pillars define who we are and how we serve our students and community every day.

Our Journey

A History of Growth

From a small classroom to a thriving academic institution — the Akapaloo story.

1998

Founding of Akapaloo Academy

Established by Dr. Kwame Akapaloo with 3 teachers and 48 students, with a vision to make quality education accessible to all families in the region.

2003

First Graduating Class & National Recognition

Our inaugural Senior Secondary graduating class achieved a 100% pass rate. The school received its first national award for academic excellence.

2008

New Campus & Science Block Opening

A major expansion saw the completion of our state-of-the-art science block, ICT lab, and 500-seat auditorium.

2014

International Accreditation

Akapaloo Academy achieved full international accreditation, enabling graduates to directly apply to universities worldwide.

2019

STEM Centre of Excellence Designation

Recognised by the Ministry of Education as a national STEM Centre of Excellence — one of only five schools to receive this designation.

2024

Best School Award & 2,400 Students

Celebrating 26 years of excellence with over 2,400 enrolled students, 180 staff members, and another regional Best School Award.
