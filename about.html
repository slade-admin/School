Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Regional Excellence

Founded in 1985 by the visionary educator Nana Kofi Akapaloo, the school began as a small community secondary school serving the Upper East Region of Ghana with only 120 students and 8 teachers.

Over four decades, through unwavering commitment to quality education, Akapaloo Academy has grown into a prestigious institution enrolling over 2,400 students and employing more than 120 dedicated educators and support staff.

Today, our alumni can be found in leading universities, hospitals, boardrooms, and government offices across Ghana and around the world — a testament to the Academy's enduring legacy of excellence.

To provide a high-quality, inclusive education that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel academically, contribute positively to society, and lead purposeful lives in an ever-changing world.
