Admissions

Begin your child's journey to excellence. We welcome students from all backgrounds who are ready to learn, grow, and lead.

How to Apply

Simple 5-Step Application Process

Applying to Akapaloo Academy is straightforward. Follow these steps to secure your child's place.

1

Complete Application

Fill out the online or paper application form below. All fields are required.

2

Submit Documents

Provide supporting documents including previous school reports and birth certificate.

3

Entrance Assessment

Students sit a brief academic assessment tailored to their entry grade level.

4

Interview & Tour

Applicants and parents are invited for a brief interview and a guided school tour.

5

Offer & Enrolment

Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter and proceed to pay enrolment fees.

What You Need

Admission Requirements

Please prepare the following documents before submitting your application.

📋

Completed Application Form

Download and complete the PDF form, or fill the online form below. One form per student.

📄

Previous School Reports (last 2 years)

Official academic transcripts or end-of-year report cards from the applicant's current or most recent school.

🪪

Birth Certificate

Original or certified copy of the applicant's birth certificate for age verification.

🖼️

Passport-Sized Photographs (2)

Recent coloured photographs of the applicant on a plain white or blue background.

💉

Immunisation Records

Up-to-date vaccination card or certificate issued by a recognised health authority.

📝

Recommendation Letter (Senior Secondary only)

One recommendation letter from the applicant's current class teacher or head teacher.

Tuition & Fees

School Fees 2025/2026

Transparent, competitive fees that reflect the exceptional quality of education at Akapaloo Academy.

Level Grades Termly Fee Annual Fee Boarding (Optional)
Primary School 1–6 GHS 2,800 GHS 8,400 GHS 1,200/term
Junior Secondary 7–9 GHS 3,400 GHS 10,200 GHS 1,500/term
Senior Secondary 10–12 GHS 4,200 GHS 12,600 GHS 1,800/term
💡

Payment Notes

Fees are payable at the beginning of each term. A 5% sibling discount applies for the second and subsequent children enrolled simultaneously. Scholarships are available for academically gifted students from low-income households — ask the Admissions Office for details.
