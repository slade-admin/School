How to Apply

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to complete your application to Akapaloo Academy.

1

Review Requirements

Check the eligibility criteria and required documents for your chosen programme of study.

2

Complete the Application Form

Fill in the online application form below with accurate student and parent/guardian information.

3

Submit Supporting Documents

Attach certified copies of BECE results, birth certificate, and two passport photographs.

4

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee of GHS 50 via Mobile Money or bank transfer.

5

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants will be invited for a written assessment and brief interview.

6

Admission Offer

Successful applicants will receive an official admission letter via email and post within 2 weeks.

Eligibility

Entry Requirements

All applicants must meet the following requirements for consideration.

🔬 Science Programme

  • Aggregate 6–12 in BECE
  • Grade B3 or above in Mathematics
  • Grade B3 or above in Integrated Science
  • Grade B3 or above in English Language
  • Minimum age: 14 years at time of entry

📊 Business Programme

  • Aggregate 6–18 in BECE
  • Grade C5 or above in Mathematics
  • Grade C5 or above in English Language
  • Grade C5 or above in Social Studies
  • Minimum age: 14 years at time of entry

🎨 General Arts Programme

  • Aggregate 6–24 in BECE
  • Grade C6 or above in English Language
  • Grade C6 or above in Social Studies
  • Good performance in History or RME
  • Minimum age: 14 years at time of entry

📋 All Applicants

  • Certified copy of BECE results slip
  • Certified copy of birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • School leaving certificate from JHS
  • Parent/guardian national ID copy
