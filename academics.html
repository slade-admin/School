Academic Programmes

A rigorous, well-rounded curriculum designed to challenge every student and prepare them for a bright future.

Levels of Study

From Foundation to Graduation

Akapaloo Academy offers a seamless 12-year educational journey across three school levels.

🌱

Primary School

Grades 1–6 · Ages 6–12

Our primary programme builds strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies through a blend of structured lessons and exploratory learning activities.

English Language Mathematics Science Social Studies Creative Arts Physical Education
📐

Junior Secondary

Grades 7–9 · Ages 13–15

Students encounter a wider range of disciplines — including foreign languages, integrated sciences, pre-vocational skills, and ICT — building a broad base for senior secondary specialisation.

Integrated Science Mathematics ICT French History Pre-Vocational
🎓

Senior Secondary

Grades 10–12 · Ages 16–18

Students choose from three specialised tracks — Science, Humanities/Arts, or Business — with elective options. Graduates are prepared for university entrance and professional careers globally.

Science Track Arts & Humanities Business Studies Advanced Maths Research Methods

Our Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Learning happens best in inspiring, well-equipped spaces. Here is what Akapaloo Academy offers.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Three fully equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology — each designed to international safety and pedagogical standards.

💻

ICT Lab & Smart Classrooms

100-station computer lab with high-speed internet, plus 30 smart classrooms with interactive whiteboards across all levels.

📚

Library & Resource Centre

Over 15,000 titles, e-books access, periodicals, and dedicated study and research spaces for students and staff.

🏟️

Sports Complex

Regulation football pitch, basketball and tennis courts, a 200m athletics track, and an indoor sports hall.

🎭

500-Seat Auditorium

A professional performance venue for assemblies, drama productions, concerts, and school events.

🍽️

Dining & Wellness Centre

Modern cafeteria serving balanced, nutritious meals; health clinic staffed by a resident nurse and visiting doctor.
