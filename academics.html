Curriculum Overview

Academic Structure at Akapaloo Academy

Our Senior High School programme runs over three years (Forms 1–3), aligned with the Ghana Education Service curriculum and assessed through WASSCE examinations.

📖

Core Subjects

All students study English, Mathematics, Integrated Science, Social Studies, and Religious & Moral Education as core subjects.

🔎

Elective Programmes

Students choose a specialist programme (Science, Business, Arts, STEM, Agriculture) and take three elective subjects within that track.

🏅

WASSCE Examinations

Students sit the West African Senior School Certificate Examination in Form 3 and our historically consistent A & B pass rate exceeds 98%.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports & Athletics

Football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, and swimming. Our teams regularly compete at regional and national championships.

Debate & Public Speaking

Our award-winning debate club has produced national champions and develops critical thinking and confident communication skills.

Drama & Performing Arts
